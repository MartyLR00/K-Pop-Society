Learn Korean

The Korean language, known as 한국어 (hangugeo) in South Korea and 조선말 (chosŏnmal) in North Korea, is the official language of both countries. It's spoken by about 82 million people and is part of the Koreanic language family. Korean has a unique writing system called Hangul, which was invented in the 15th century. The language is recognized for its distinct phonetic alphabet and grammatical structure, differing notably between the two Koreas due to different standardizations1.
